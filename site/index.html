Developer tool for TS / JS codebases

Understand any codebase in 10 seconds

Memor projects your codebase into four interactive views — structure, flow, and impact — so you can see what happens, what matters, and what could break.

$ npx memor Copy

What happens

See the runtime flow of your codebase — how requests move, which systems activate, and in what order.

What matters

Instantly identify architecturally central systems, high-traffic paths, and critical dependencies.

What breaks

Understand the blast radius of any change before you make it. See which systems are at risk and why.

Four views

Every angle of your architecture

Each view answers a different question. Together, they give you complete operational clarity.

Overview

Identity, key signals, entry points, and architecture zones — everything you need in one glance.

Structure

Interactive system graph with connections, types, and hierarchy. See how everything connects.

Flow

Animated storytelling of runtime execution paths. Each step shows systems involved, files, and fused impact intelligence.

Impact

Change any system and see the ripple. Blast radius, risk levels, and concrete consequences — before you ship.

How it works

Three commands. Zero config.

Memor analyzes your codebase locally and opens an interactive visualization in your browser. No cloud. No setup.

1

Navigate to your project

Open your terminal and cd into any TypeScript or JavaScript project.

$ cd your-project
2

Run Memor

One command. No install needed — npx handles it.

$ npx memor
3

Understand

Your browser opens with four interactive views. Explore structure, flow, and impact instantly.
